About Me

A Personal Journey of Care and Community

Serving Solamar Since 2019

For over five years, I've been part of the Solamar community, and in that time, I've come to understand just how much care, connection, and help really matter here. While living here, I cared for my mom, Jacqy Bussio, alongside my father — an experience that opened my eyes to the everyday challenges many seniors face. That insight is what inspired me to create Solamar Care.

Whether it's helping William Fond with tech support, TV upgrades, meal prep and medications, ensuring Peggy had her weekly groceries ordered, or simply being there when Harvey fell and laid on the floor calling out for help — I've seen firsthand how much of a difference it makes to have a trusted neighbor close by. I've also built relationships, like daily walks with Rick, that remind me how many people here just want someone to talk to.

Solamar Care isn't just a service — it's a commitment to showing up, being present, and offering help from someone who truly cares about this community and its members.
